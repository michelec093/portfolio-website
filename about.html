My Story

I‘m an aspiring UI designer who has mostly worked as a graphic designer in the commercial printing industry. I am currently working as a Visual Merchandising Coordinator for Five Below in Philadelphia, working hand in hand with the Marketing & Design department. My ambitions to work in the UI field is rooted in the fact that I love to learn and print is becoming a thing of the past. Clients need a partner who is up on the latest technology to help advertise their business. I want transition from print to tech so I can use my skills to help create the UX/UI interfaces that will make people’s lives easier.
